Facts About Glasgow

  • It's a UNESCO City of Music
  • It has the third-oldest subway system in the world
  • The ultrasound was invented here in 1956
  • Oasis got their big break after playing here in 1993
  • There is always a traffic cone on the Duke of Wellington statue!

Facts About Manchester

  • Home of the World Pie-Eating Championships
  • The first passenger railway train set of from here in 1830
  • The first computer with stored program and memory is located here
  • The Suffragette movement started here
  • The vegetarian movement started here in 1847

Facts About Birmingham

  • There are more canals here (35 miles) than in Venice (26 miles)
  • The world's first football league started here
  • The postage stamp was invented here in 1839
  • Home to the 5th busiest McDonald's in the world
  • The board game Cluedo was invented here in 1943

Facts About London

  • The oldest building in London is almost 1800 years old!
  • Holds the record for most Olympic Games hosted by a city
  • The shortest street is only 36ft long
  • Has so many trees that it meets the UN's definition of a forest

Facts About Cape Town

  • Winemaking in the region dates back to 1659 - the oldest vineyards outside Europe!
  • Hosted the first FIFA World Cup match held in Africa in 2010
  • Home to a colony of endangered penguins
  • Has 7000 species of plant which aren't found anywhere else in the world
  • The world's first heart transplant took place here in 1967
